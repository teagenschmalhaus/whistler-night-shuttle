# Whistler Night Shuttle — Business Context

## Overview
Whistler Night Shuttle is a private shuttle service providing reliable late-night transportation between Vancouver International Airport (YVR) and Whistler, BC, with optional stops in Squamish along the scenic Sea to Sky Highway. The company is powered by Whistler Resort Cabs.

## Contact
- **Email:** whistlernightshuttle@gmail.com
- **Phone:** 778-200-9610
- **Website:** https://www.whistlernightshuttle.com

## Service Details
- **Route:** Vancouver Airport (YVR) ↔ Whistler, BC (with optional stop in Squamish)
- **Vehicle:** Spacious van accommodating up to 7 passengers
- **Hours:** 24/7 (primarily focused on late-night hours: 10pm to 8am)
- **Service type:** Private, door-to-door

### Key Features
- Up to 7 passengers per vehicle
- Flexible pick-up and drop-off locations
- Optional stop in Squamish (upon request, at reduced cost)
- No surge pricing
- Late-night availability
- Professional drivers
- Vehicle matched to group size

## Pricing
- **Vancouver ↔ Whistler (one way):** $300 CAD
- **Flat rate:** $360 CAD (tax included)
  - Includes: door-to-door service, professional driver, vehicle matched to group size, no surge pricing, late-night availability
- **Squamish transport:** Reduced rate — contact for details

## Booking
- Online booking available via the website
- Booking form fields: full name, phone number, email, pick-up location, drop-off location, date & time, number of people, luggage count, flight number

## Locations Served
- Vancouver (YVR Airport)
- Squamish, BC
- Whistler, BC

## Customer Reviews
Customers highlight smooth rides, friendly drivers, comfortable vans, and appreciation for the flexible Squamish stop option.

## Competitive Positioning
Whistler Night Shuttle fills a niche for late-night travelers (10pm–8am) who arrive at YVR when other shuttle services (YVR Skylynx, Whistler Shuttle, Epic Rides) have limited or no availability. It offers a private, fixed-price alternative to shared shuttles.

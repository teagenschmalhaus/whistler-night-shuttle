FAQ

Common questions

How much does a shuttle from Whistler to YVR cost?

Our flat rate is $360 CAD, tax included, for up to 4 passengers. No surge pricing, no hidden fees — ever.

How early should I leave Whistler to catch my flight?

The drive from Whistler to YVR takes approximately 2 to 2.5 hours along the Sea to Sky Highway. We recommend allowing 3 hours before your departure time to account for check-in and security.

Do you pick up directly from my Whistler accommodation?

Yes — we offer door-to-door pickup from any address in Whistler Village or surrounding areas. Just provide your exact address when booking.

Is there a late-night shuttle from Whistler to Vancouver Airport?

Yes — we specialize in late-night and early-morning travel (10pm–8am) when scheduled coaches aren't running. We're available 24/7, including holidays.

Why Us

Your private shuttle from Vancouver to Whistler, any hour

Scheduled shuttles stop running after dark. Whether you need transportation from YVR to Whistler on a red-eye or a ride home after a ski weekend — we're here.

Whistler Night Shuttle is a private, door-to-door service running between Vancouver International Airport (YVR) and Whistler Village. Unlike shared buses and scheduled coaches, every ride is exclusive to your group — making us the preferred private shuttle from Vancouver to Whistler for skiers, snowboarders, and late-arriving travelers alike.

FAQ

Common questions

How much does a shuttle from YVR to Whistler cost?

Our flat rate is $360 CAD, tax included, for up to 4 passengers. No surge pricing, no hidden fees — ever.

How long is the drive from Vancouver Airport to Whistler?

The drive along the Sea to Sky Highway takes approximately 2 to 2.5 hours depending on road and weather conditions.

Do you offer late-night transportation from YVR to Whistler?

Yes — we specialize in late-night travel (10pm–8am) when scheduled shuttle services aren't running. We're available 24/7, including holidays.

Is this a private shuttle or a shared bus?

It's fully private. Your group gets the vehicle — no shared stops, no waiting for strangers. Door-to-door from Vancouver Airport to your exact Whistler address.

Why Us

Your private shuttle from Vancouver to Squamish, any hour

Scheduled services along the Sea to Sky Highway are limited — especially late at night. Whether you're arriving at YVR and heading to Squamish, or need a Squamish stop as part of a longer trip — we're here.

Whistler Night Shuttle provides private, door-to-door transportation from Vancouver International Airport (YVR) to Squamish, BC. We also offer Squamish as an add-on stop for groups heading to Whistler — perfect for splitting up a group or dropping off luggage. Every ride is exclusive to your group, with flat-rate pricing and no surprise fees.

FAQ

Common questions

How much is a shuttle from Vancouver Airport to Squamish?

Squamish pricing is reduced from our standard Whistler rate. Contact us directly at 778-200-9610 or whistlernightshuttle@gmail.com for a quick quote.

How long is the drive from YVR to Squamish?

The drive from Vancouver Airport (YVR) to Squamish along the Sea to Sky Highway takes approximately 1 to 1.5 hours depending on traffic and conditions.

Can you drop off in Squamish on the way to Whistler?

Yes — we offer a Squamish stop as an add-on to any YVR to Whistler trip. Contact us to arrange pickup or drop-off in Squamish at a reduced rate.

Do you offer late-night shuttles to Squamish from Vancouver?

Yes — we're available 24/7, including late-night and early-morning runs to Squamish from Vancouver Airport (YVR).

# Operations & Logistics

## Booking Process

1. **Customer submits form** on the website with their trip details (name, phone, email, pick-up/drop-off location, date & time, number of people, luggage count, flight number).
2. **We respond via email** to confirm the details and ask the customer to confirm their booking.
3. **Once confirmed**, we send a **Stripe payment link** for a **$0.50 reservation hold** to secure the booking.
4. **After payment**, we notify our partner **Whistler Resort Cabs** to fulfill the ride.

## Payment
- Platform: **Stripe**
- Reservation hold: **$0.50 CAD**
- Remaining balance handled through Whistler Resort Cabs fulfillment

## Partners
- **Whistler Resort Cabs** — fulfillment partner responsible for dispatching and completing rides

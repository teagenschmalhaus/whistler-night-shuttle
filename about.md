# About Whistler Night Shuttle

Whistler Night Shuttle is a private transportation service built for travelers who need a reliable, comfortable ride between Vancouver International Airport (YVR) and Whistler, BC — especially late at night when most other shuttle services aren't running.

Powered by Whistler Resort Cabs, we operate 24/7 with a core focus on the late-night window (10pm–8am), ensuring you always have a ride no matter when your flight lands or departs. Our route runs along the stunning Sea to Sky Highway, with optional stops in Squamish available upon request.

## What We Offer

- **Private van rides** for up to 7 passengers
- **Door-to-door service** — flexible pick-up and drop-off locations
- **Optional Squamish stop** at a reduced rate
- **No surge pricing** — what you see is what you pay
- **Professional, friendly drivers**
- **24/7 availability**, with a specialization in late-night travel

## Pricing

- **Vancouver ↔ Whistler (one way):** $300 CAD
- **Flat rate (tax included):** $360 CAD
- **Squamish transport:** Reduced rate — contact us for details

## Who We Serve

We fill the gap left by larger, scheduled shuttle services that stop running after dark. Whether you're a skier arriving on a red-eye, a group heading home after a long weekend in Whistler, or a traveler who just needs a dependable late-night ride — Whistler Night Shuttle is here for you.
